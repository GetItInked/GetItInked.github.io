Meet Rohit Jaiswal

Welcome to Get It Inked, where passion for art meets skin. I'm Rohit Jaiswal, and with years of experience and a commitment to unique, high-quality tattoos, I strive to create pieces you'll love for a lifetime.

I specialize in styles like black & grey, realism, neo-traditional, and more. Every tattoo is a collaboration, ensuring your vision is perfectly captured. Hygiene and client comfort are my top priorities.
